Channel mix & new launches

Google Search

  • CTR held at 7.1% across Q1 — above industry (~6.7%).
  • Touhy & Harlem is the volume leader (126 clicks in Mar).
  • West Loop is the conversion leader (11.67% conv. rate in Mar).

Display / Pmax

  • Switched from Pmax → Display campaign on Feb 20.
  • Delivered 4 leads in Mar (3 West Loop, 1 Touhy & Harlem).
  • CTR normalized to 0.76% — on target (0.5–1%).

Meta Ads (NEW)

  • Launched Mar 23 — 5 leads in the first week.
  • Elmhurst Open House creative drove 3 of 5 leads.
  • Cheapest channel so far: $49.13 / lead.

Key takeaways & next steps

What's working

  • Cost efficiency is trending down — blended CPL fell 40% from Jan to Mar.
  • Mount Prospect responded best to keyword optimization (1 → 3 leads + phone call).
  • Meta Ads launch is the cheapest channel out of the gate ($49/lead).
  • SEO rankings climbed from position 30 → 12 YoY despite lower traffic.

Priorities for April (from Mar report)

  • Push new "Book a tour" landing pages into production to lift conversion rate.
  • Test video ads on Meta and Google to reduce CPL further.
  • Expand marketing to additional locations (Lincoln Square, Irving Park showing early AI-chat interest).
  • Close the West Loop competitive gap vs. Goddard / Brightpath.
