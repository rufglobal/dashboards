What We've Learned (10 Days of Data)

What Drives Results

  • Community question threads — Sauglasville restaurant recs (101 score) drove 34K page views in one day
  • Hyper-local groups 6-80K — Fennville Informed (0.66% eng rate), Hamilton Happenings (0.61%)
  • Guest Guide solicitation posts — 10 suggestions received, businesses reacting to their own mentions
  • ISO reply pipeline — 5 warm leads in 4 days, 2 ranked #1 Most Relevant
  • Pool + fire content — Pool avg score 32, fire pit 26.6 — our strongest visual assets
  • Downtown SH Chatline — 196 reactions, 30 shares from one neighbor-tone intro post

What Doesn't Work

  • Large groups 200K+ — Day Trips MI (743K), Abandoned Places (420K) — declined or zero engagement
  • Links in post body — Facebook throttles reach. Links go in first comment only.
  • Cold posts to new groups — Every post without prior comments was declined or removed
  • Listing-style posts — Avg score 1 vs community question avg score 52
  • X engagement via API — 403 blocked for cold replies (free tier). Browser-based solution needed.
  • Stale engagement data — CSV records engagement at posting time, not after. Needs weekly refresh.
