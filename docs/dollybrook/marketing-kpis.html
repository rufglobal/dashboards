What We've Learned — 10 Days of Data

What Drives Results

  • Community question threads — avg score 52/post. Sauglasville (101 score) drove 34K page views
  • Guest Guide solicitation — avg score 26/post. Businesses react to their own mentions
  • Neighbor intro in local groups — SH Chatline: 196 reactions, 30 shares from one post
  • Small groups 6-80K — 0.4-0.66% engagement rate vs 0% in 200K+ groups
  • ISO reply pipeline — 5 leads in 4 days, 2 at #1 Most Relevant
  • Pool + fire content — Pool avg score 32, firepit 26.6
  • Real video over AI — pickleball reel with real footage posted to 2 groups tonight

What Doesn't Work

  • Listing-style posts — avg score 1/post. Stop doing these in community groups
  • Large groups 200K+ — declined or zero engagement every time
  • Links in post body — Facebook throttles reach. First comment only.
  • Cold posts without prior comments — 100% decline rate
  • X engagement via API — 403 blocked. Needs browser login.
  • Stale CSV data — engagement logged at post time, not refreshed
  • AI-generated video for pickleball — players on wrong sides, night scenes. Real footage wins.
