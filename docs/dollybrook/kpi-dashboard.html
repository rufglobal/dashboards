Learnings

✅ What Drives Results

  • Community question threads — avg score 52/post. Sauglasville drove 34K page views
  • Guest Guide solicitation — avg score 26/post. Businesses react to their own mentions
  • Neighbor intro in local groups — SH Chatline: 196 reactions, 30 shares from one post
  • Small groups 6-80K — 0.4-0.66% engagement rate vs 0% in 200K+ groups
  • ISO reply pipeline — 5 leads in 4 days, 1 converted ($$$)
  • Pool + fire content — Pool avg score 32, firepit 26.6 (top content types)
  • Album posts — avg 19.8 reactions vs 13.6 for single photos
  • New amenity announcements — consistently 40-63 reactions all-time
  • Real video over AI-generated — authentic footage wins every time

❌ What Doesn't Work

  • Listing-style posts — avg score 1/post. Stop in community groups
  • Large groups 200K+ — declined or zero engagement every time
  • Links in post body — Facebook throttles reach. Link in first comment OR soft format (no https://)
  • Cold posts without prior comments — 100% decline rate
  • AI-generated video for pickleball — players on wrong sides, night scenes. Real footage wins
  • Posts with external links — avg 6.0 reactions vs 14.6 without. Never lead with a link
  • Native templates / AI graphics — avg 7.7 reactions. Authentic photos beat designed graphics
  • No-photo text posts — avg 8.1 reactions vs 15.0 with photo
