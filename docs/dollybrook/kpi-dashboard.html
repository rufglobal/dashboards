What We've Learned — 16 Days of Data

✅ What Drives Results

  • Community question threads — avg score 52/post. Sauglasville (101 score) drove 34K page views
  • Guest Guide solicitation — avg score 26/post. Businesses react to their own mentions
  • Neighbor intro in local groups — SH Chatline: 196 reactions, 30 shares from one post
  • Small groups 6–82K — 0.4–0.66% engagement rate vs 0% in 200K+ groups
  • ISO reply pipeline — 5 leads in 4 days, 2 at #1 Most Relevant
  • Pool + fire content — Pool avg score 32, firepit 26.6 (Facebook historical data)
  • Real video over AI — Pickleball reel with real footage outperforms AI-generated
  • DOM.setFileInputFiles photo upload — Confirmed working Apr 15 ✅ No OS picker

❌ What Doesn't Work

  • Listing-style posts — avg score 1/post. No community value = no engagement
  • Large groups 200K+ — declined or zero engagement every time
  • Links in post body — Facebook throttles reach. First comment only
  • Cold posts without prior comments — 100% decline rate
  • Illinois Fun & Finds (102K) — No post composer available for DollyBrook Page
  • Subagents with no fallback logic — Getting stuck on one blocked group burns budget. Need skip-after-2-attempts rule
  • AI-generated pickleball video — Players on wrong sides, night scenes. Real footage only
