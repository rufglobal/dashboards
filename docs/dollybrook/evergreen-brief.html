1. What Is DollyBrook?

DollyBrook Family Resort is a 34-acre private resort in Fennville, Michigan — 10 minutes from Saugatuck, 2 hours from Chicago, 1–1.5 hours from Grand Rapids.

It has 9 themed cabins that can sleep up to 46 guests total. Guests rent individual cabins or buy out the entire resort. It is not a hotel — it's an exclusive, private-feeling property where your group is typically the only guests on-site.

DollyBrook has been operating for 13+ years and has strong social proof: 4.8 stars Google · 98% Facebook recommendations · 5 stars Yelp.

7. Marketing Strategy (Evergreen Principles)

The Core Funnel

Content → Reaction → Follower invite → Follower → Booking

Mihai invites every person who reacts to a DollyBrook post. This is the primary follower acquisition engine. Every post should be optimized to maximize reactions.

What Content Works (from 534-post historical analysis)

1. New amenity announcements — #1 all-time. Pool opening, hot tubs, string lights, pickleball = 40–63 reactions.

2. Pool content — avg engagement score 32.0 (highest by content type)

3. Fire pit / evening scenes — avg score 26.6

4. Family content — avg score 26.2, highest share rate

5. Local area (Saugatuck / MI) — avg score 23.5

6. Cabin features — avg score 23.4

7. Giveaways — highest single-post scores ever (362+)

8. Behind-the-scenes / property improvements — 37–42 reactions consistently

9. Album posts (multi-photo) — avg 19.8 reactions vs 13.6 for single photos

What Doesn't Work (proven by data)

  • Posts with external links in the body: avg 6.0 reactions (vs 14.6 without). Platforms suppress outbound links.
  • AI-generated graphics / native templates: avg 7.7 reactions. Authentic photos win.
  • Text-only posts: avg 8.1 reactions vs 15.0 with photo.
  • Listing-style "here's our cabin" posts in community groups — nearly zero engagement.
  • Cold posts (no prior relationship with group) — very high rejection rate.

Content Pillars

1. Community Guide — DollyBrook as local expert, not just a business

2. Pool Season — #1 content driver, opens Memorial Day

3. 13-Year Story — authenticity, family business, neighbor tone

4. Pickleball / Sports — emerging audience, 11 groups in pipeline

5. Influencers / Girls' Trip — in early development

6. Corporate Retreats — high-value, full buyout play

7. Feeder Cities — Chicago, Grand Rapids, Milwaukee, Indianapolis

8. Place Before Product — Saugatuck/Michigan area first, DollyBrook second

Social Proof (always include in STR / corporate posts)

4.8 stars Google · 98% Facebook recommendations · 5 stars Yelp · 13 years in business

ISO Reply Formula (accommodation requests)

When someone posts "looking for a cabin in Michigan for X dates":

1. Acknowledge their specific vibe/group personally

2. Be honest about availability upfront

3. List amenities matching their exact ask (bullet points with checkmarks)

4. Mention nearby local spots relevant to their group

5. End with "even if not this year, save this for future"

6. Include booking link directly in the reply (not as a first comment)

Posting Rules

  • Always use https:// before links
  • Short hyphens - not em dashes —
  • End posts with 4–6 relevant hashtags
  • Post timing: 6–8 PM ET performs best
  • Keep captions SHORT — long captions suppress reactions
  • End every post with a reaction prompt ("❤️ if you'd rather wake up here")
  • 80% value / 20% promo — ALL posts reaction-optimized
  • Never lead with a link — platforms suppress reach
