What Works vs What Doesn't

What Works

  • Small community groups (6K–80K) — local feel, admin trust, thread potential
  • Hyper-local neighbor angle — "we're in Fennville" beats any promo pitch
  • Posts that spark threads — one 26-comment thread = 34K page views
  • ISO reply pipeline — warm leads convert far better than cold posts
  • Pool + firepit photos — score 32.0 and 26.6 respectively
  • Relationship capital first — comment before posting = admin approval

What Doesn't Work

  • Large groups 200K+ — strict moderation, zero thread potential
  • Links in post body — Facebook throttles reach, higher decline rate
  • Cold posts with no relationship — declined or removed every time
  • Burst posting, then silence — Facebook rewards weekly consistency
  • Nostalgia groups — no promo tolerance, wrong audience
  • Wrong-fit ISO replies — lakefront/dock required = skip entirely
