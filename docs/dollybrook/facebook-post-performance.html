💡 What Works vs What Doesn't

✅ What Works

  • Small community groups (6K–80K) — local feel, admin trust, community threads
  • Hyper-local neighbor angle — "we're right here in Fennville" beats any promo pitch
  • Posts that spark threads — ask a local question, invite conversation. One 26-comment thread = 34K page views
  • ISO reply pipeline — warm lead replies convert far better than cold posts
  • Pool + firepit photos — top engagement types (score 32 and 26.6)
  • Comments before first post — relationship capital = admin approval

❌ What Doesn't Work

  • Large groups 200K+ — strict moderation, cold posts declined, zero thread momentum
  • Links in post body — Facebook throttles reach, higher decline rate
  • Cold posts with no relationship — declined or removed every time
  • Wide posting bursts — 10 groups in a day then silence for days
  • Nostalgia groups — no promo tolerance, wrong audience
  • Wrong-fit ISOs — lakefront + dock required = skip, don't reply
